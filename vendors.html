Tariq Electric (Pvt) Ltd

Tariq Electric is the official transformer vendor of Hello Sun Energy, ensuring top-quality and efficient power systems

Established in 1993, Tariq Electric is one of Pakistan’s leading electrical engineering companies, specializing in the design and manufacturing of dry-type and oil-type transformers, switchgear, and power distribution panels. With decades of experience, the company is trusted nationwide for its reliable and innovative energy solutions. As an approved vendor for WAPDA, K-Electric, and a licensed ABB panel builder, Tariq Electric continues to deliver excellence in every product.
